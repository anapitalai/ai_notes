# ai_bible_study

A collection of notes and resources exploring the intersection of Artificial Intelligence and Bible study.

## Overview

This section contains AI-assisted tools, notes, and insights designed to deepen understanding of scripture through modern AI techniques such as natural language processing, semantic search, and summarization.

## Topics

- **Scripture Analysis** – Using AI to analyze themes, patterns, and language in the Bible
- **Semantic Search** – Finding related passages across the Old and New Testaments
- **Summarization** – AI-generated summaries of books and chapters
- **Question & Answer** – Conversational AI for Bible-related questions
- **Cross-Reference Discovery** – Automatically identifying scripture cross-references

## Getting Started

Browse the notes in this directory to explore how AI can be applied to Bible study. Each file covers a specific topic or technique.

## Resources

- [Hugging Face](https://huggingface.co/) – Open-source NLP models and datasets
